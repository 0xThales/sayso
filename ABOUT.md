# sayso

**Respondents talk. You get structured data.**

Sayso is a voice-first form builder. Instead of asking people to type answers into boxes, you send them a link and they have a short, natural conversation with a voice agent. The agent asks your questions, listens, follows up when something is unclear, and hands you back clean, structured data — the same rows and columns you'd get from a traditional form, just with far more signal behind every answer.

---

## Why voice

Typed forms are a compromise. People rush through them, skip the open-ended questions, and give you two-word answers where you wanted a paragraph. The response rate is fine; the *quality* is thin.

Talking is different. People say more when they speak than when they type. They explain, they go on tangents, they tell you things you didn't think to ask. A good interviewer knows how to catch those moments and pull on the thread. Sayso is that interviewer — available 24/7, infinitely patient, and consistent across every respondent.

You get the completion rate of a form with the depth of a user interview.

---

## How it works

1. **Build the form.** Write your questions the way you'd ask them out loud. No logic trees, no branching rules — the agent handles follow-ups on its own.
2. **Pick a voice.** Choose the personality that fits your brand. Emma is warm, curious, and gentle. Tim is direct, witty, and efficient. More voices coming.
3. **Share the link.** Respondents open it in any browser, tap to start, and talk. No app, no signup, no friction.
4. **Get the data.** Answers land in your dashboard as structured fields — plus the full transcript, a summary, and a quality score for every conversation.

The agent knows when an answer is vague and asks for more. It knows when someone is done and moves on. It stays on-topic, in your brand voice, and never loses the thread.

---

## What you get back

Every completed conversation gives you four things:

- **Structured answers** — the fields you defined, filled in cleanly, ready to export or pipe into anything.
- **Full transcript** — every word, timestamped. Search it, quote it, share it.
- **Auto-summary** — a short readout of what the respondent said and meant.
- **Quality evaluation** — did the agent cover everything? Were the answers clear? Was the respondent comfortable? You see the signal and the noise, separately.

Nothing gets lost. Nothing gets guessed.

## The philosophy

We think forms got stuck. They optimized for the machine — easy to parse, easy to store — at the expense of the person filling them out. Voice flips that. It optimizes for the human, and lets the machine do the hard work of turning a conversation into a row in a database.

Sayso isn't trying to replace interviews. It's trying to make the best parts of an interview — the listening, the follow-up, the *"tell me more about that"* — available at the scale of a form.

Respondents talk. You get structured data. That's the whole idea.
